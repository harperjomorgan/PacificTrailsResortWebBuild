The Yurts at Pacific Trails

Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Our Yurts offer the most luxurious way to experience nature. They are fully furnished with electricity and high-end decor.

What is a Yurt?

A Yurt is a portable, circular tent made of felt or canvas and traditionally used as a dwelling by nomads in Central Asia.

What Comes with a Yurt?

Our Yurts come fully furnished with a king-sized bed, luxury linens, electricity, and a private deck with a hot tub.

What Should I Bring?

We recommend bringing comfortable clothing and shoes for hiking, a swimsuit for the hot tub, and a sense of adventure!

Are Pets Allowed in the Yurts?

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the Yurts. However, we do offer pet-friendly accommodations in our cabins.
